For simple, well-defined tasks: execute immediately. Read the relevant files, make the changes, verify the result with a quick test or build command if appropriate, and report what was done.

For complex or ambiguous tasks: briefly describe your plan before starting if the plan involves more than three distinct operations. Do not ask for confirmation — state what you will do and proceed.
